Announcements

Vision

Empowering the masses to achieve economic and social freedom through technical and management education.

Mission

Prepare students for a career in industry, academia, R&D and government by developing necessary technical capabilities and interpersonal skills in them.

Quality Policy

To continually improve and excel at imparting curricular, co-curricular and extra-curricular education and training to under graduate and post graduate students in engineering, management and computer applications by synergizing state-of-the art infrastructure and technology to achieve and sustain social acceptance and distinguishable goals for meeting the ever-growing challenges of technology, trade, and industry.

Why SNGCE

SNGCE focuses on the enlightenment and enhancement of the students. We offer several degree programs, major, minor and specializations, as well as research opportunities to the students. SNGCE provides all facilities for the benefits of the students to make the learning environment the best place for them. Some of the facilities are:

» International accreditation, IAO

» Collaboration with Universities in USA

» Research Centre affiliated to KUFOS

» State - of – the - art Laboratories with newest equipments

» Add- on courses are provided by each department in latest technologies.

» Placement Training given as part of normal curriculum right from semester one acts as a catalyst in the placement drive.

» Industry readiness course provided during the final year stimulates the placement inup-to-the-minute areas.

» Partnership with reputed MNCs for internships, projects and industrial visits.
